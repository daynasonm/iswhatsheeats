Percy Jackson and the Olympians is about Percy, a 12-year-old who discovers he’s a demigod and the son of Poseidon. Thrust into a hidden world of Greek gods, monsters, and ancient prophecies, Percy embarks on quests to stop war among the gods, recover lost relics, and protect his friends. Along the way, he learns the cost of loyalty, the truth about his powers, and the weight of destiny.
