BOOK

Diary of a Wimpy Kid by Jeff Kinney follows middle schooler Greg Heffley as he navigates the awkward, funny, and often embarrassing challenges of growing up. Told through his diary, complete with doodles and sarcastic commentary, Greg tries to gain popularity while avoiding blame for his mishaps. The book humorously captures the ups and downs of adolescence and friendship.
