BOOK

Rich Dad Poor Dad contrasts the financial mindsets of Robert Kiyosaki’s two father figures—his educated but financially struggling “poor dad” and his entrepreneurial, wealth-savvy “rich dad.” The book emphasizes financial literacy, investing, and building passive income over traditional job security. Its core message: wealth comes from how you think about money, not how much you earn.
