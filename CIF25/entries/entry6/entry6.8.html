Papa, Please Get the Moon for Me by Eric Carle is a gentle story about a young girl named Monica who wants to play with the moon. Her father climbs a long ladder to bring it down, only to find that the moon must shrink before he can carry it back. The book blends imagination and science, showing the moon’s changing phases in a touching tale of love and wonder.
