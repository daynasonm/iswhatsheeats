The Hunger Games follows Katniss Everdeen, a teenager living in a dystopian society where children are forced to fight to the death in a televised arena. When she volunteers to take her sister’s place, Katniss must rely on her instincts, resilience, and strategy to survive. Her defiance sparks a movement that begins to challenge the oppressive system controlling their world.
