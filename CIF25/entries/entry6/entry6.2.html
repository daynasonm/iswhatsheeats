BOOK

Factfulness by Hans Rosling challenges common misconceptions about the world, showing that many people have an outdated and overly negative view of global progress. Using data, it reveals that health, education, and income levels have improved dramatically worldwide. The book encourages thinking based on facts—not fear—and promotes a more accurate, hopeful perspective on humanity’s future.
